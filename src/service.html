  • Intervals

    A Shuttle Every 15–25 minutes!

    Shuttles on the Orange, Pruple, and Green Route have a 15–20 minute interval.

    The Banner route has a shuttle en route every 20–25 minutes: Please note that there is a 45 minute headway between 11:00am to 3:00pm for the Banner route.
